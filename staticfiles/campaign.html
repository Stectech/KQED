CAMPAIGN 21

You, and the entire Bay Area, turn to KQED for news and information that engages minds, promotes conversations, and enriches lives. But now you are changing the way you access and interact with media, and we must change right along with you to continue to be the Bay Area’s leading source for creative, critical media for curious people.

The KQED of the future will continue to be the most reliable source for original Bay Area content. Our strength lies in how well we serve our local audiences with the information and tools they need to participate in civic life, contribute to our vibrant culture and pursue 21st century careers. Our sustainability depends upon innovation as we transition our current successful support model to new digital media.

The time is now. Join the campaign that is transforming KQED for the 21st century. Together we can ensure that KQED remains the Bay Area’s most trustworthy, relevant and essential media resource.
